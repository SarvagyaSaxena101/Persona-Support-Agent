# AdsSparkX Billing and Subscription Guide

## Subscription Tiers
AdsSparkX offers three main pricing plans tailored to different business needs:

1. **Starter Plan ($49/month):**
   - Up to 10 active campaigns.
   - Basic analytics dashboard.
   - Email support (24-hour response time).
   - Single user access.

2. **Professional Plan ($199/month):**
   - Up to 50 active campaigns.
   - Advanced AI-driven optimization insights.
   - Priority email and chat support.
   - Up to 5 team members.
   - Custom report exporting.

3. **Enterprise Plan (Custom Pricing):**
   - Unlimited campaigns.
   - Dedicated account manager.
   - 24/7 phone support and SLA guarantees.
   - Single Sign-On (SSO) integration.
   - White-labeling options.
   - Custom data retention policies.

## Cancellation Policy
Users can cancel their subscription at any time via the "Billing" tab in the dashboard. 
- Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- No partial refunds are provided for mid-month cancellations.
- Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation before permanent deletion.

## Enterprise Features
Enterprise customers have access to "Consolidated Billing," allowing multiple sub-accounts to be charged to a single corporate credit card or via wire transfer (Net-30 terms).
